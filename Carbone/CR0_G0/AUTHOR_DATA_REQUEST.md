# CR0-G0 — Brouillon de demande de données aux auteurs

## Statut

**Brouillon non envoyé.**

L'objectif est d'obtenir les données qui permettraient de réduire le pilote,
de vérifier les incertitudes et d'éviter de refaire une expérience miroir déjà
réalisée mais non publiée. L'envoi nécessite le choix d'un expéditeur et
l'accord explicite du porteur du projet.

## 1. Demande M2025 — TMSPyr

Objet : Request for raw TMSPyr kinetic and chiral-HPLC data for a preregistered mirrored replication

```text
Dear Dr Möhler and colleagues,

We are preparing a preregistered, mirrored replication of the TMSPyr Soai
system reported in your 2025 Nature Communications paper. Our narrow aim is
to compare matched reactions initialized with TMSPyr-OH of opposite absolute
configuration while keeping total alcohol and all achiral conditions fixed.

Would you be willing to share, if available:

1. the per-replicate numerical time courses and native chromatographic files
   underlying Supplementary Figures 29–39, especially Figure 31;
2. the raw calibration points, residuals and repeatability data underlying
   Supplementary Figures 27–28;
3. the method used to assign the absolute configuration of the preparatively
   separated TMSPyr-OH fractions;
4. any kinetic runs initialized with (1S)-TMSPyr-OH, including negative or
   unpublished runs;
5. timestamps, temperature records and technical-exclusion rules used for the
   three repeats at each concentration.

We would preserve the original files, hashes and attribution, and would agree
in advance on any access or publication conditions. We are also happy to
share the preregistration before data access so that the requested data cannot
silently alter the primary criterion.

Kind regards,
[name / affiliation / contact]
```

## 2. Demande Hawbaker–Blackmond

Objet : Request for raw mirrored-product data related to the 2018/2019 Soai studies

```text
Dear Dr Hawbaker and Professor Blackmond,

We are auditing whether existing Soai data contain a strictly paired mirror
experiment: equal total autocatalytic alcohol, equal-magnitude opposite ee,
identical achiral conditions, and chiral time courses retained without
post-selection.

Would you be willing to share, if available:

1. native and numerical data for the product-seeded experiments reported in
   the 2018 ACS Central Science Supporting Information, including SOA189,
   SOA190 and SOA191;
2. any corresponding experiments seeded with the S product;
3. raw records for batch H of Supplementary Table 1 in the 2019 Nature
   Chemistry study;
4. calibration, integration and technical-exclusion records needed to bound
   the uncertainty of signed product ee;
5. any negative or unpublished mirror trials relevant to this precise design.

Our intent is to preregister the encoding and separation criteria before
examining any new S-arm results. Original files would be retained with hashes
and full attribution, under access conditions agreed with you.

Kind regards,
[name / affiliation / contact]
```

## 3. Réception des données

Si des données sont reçues, elles doivent entrer dans un nouveau sous-dossier
daté, sans remplacer les sources publiques actuelles. Le message original, les
conditions d'accès, les fichiers natifs et leurs hashes sont conservés. Les
résultats S restent sous embargo jusqu'au gel de la règle confirmatoire.
